Our mission

At PixieD, we believe the future of design should be fast, intuitive, and open to everyone. In a world where visuals are key, 3D is becoming the new language of creativity. But traditional tools are holding people back. They are slow to learn, heavy to run, and built for experts.
Our mission is to break those barriers. We want to make 3D creation as easy as writing a sentence or drawing a quick sketch. With the power of AI, PixieD gives anyone the ability to turn their ideas into 3D models in just a few minutes. No long tutorials. No special training. Just your imagination, and a little magic.

How It Started

The idea for PixieD came from a shared frustration. 3D is everywhere now in games, ads, digital art and virtual spaces. But creating it still feels stuck in the past. Long hours, steep learning curves, and expensive software make it difficult for small teams and solo creators to keep up.
Our co-founder Alex Muni, an AI developer, asked a simple but powerful question. If we can generate an image from a text prompt, why not a 3D object? Together with 3D designer Lili Cori and user experience specialist Nina Vogu, he turned that question into a working prototype. What started as a spark quickly grew into a mission to make 3D design simple, accessible, and fun for everyone.

Meet the Team

PixieD was created by three people with one clear goal. Alex Muni brings deep knowledge of AI and a passion for creative technology. Lili Cori is a skilled 3D designer who knows the pain of working with clunky tools. Nina Vogu is obsessed with design that feels good to use and tells a story.
Together, they form a small but mighty team with experience in digital creation, user experience, and cutting-edge AI. They are supported by a growing group of collaborators and advisors who believe in the same vision. What unites the whole team is a drive to open up 3D to everyone, and to build a future where creativity has no limits.
